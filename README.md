# 💸 NationalDebtGPT

> The U.S. national debt is over **$34 trillion**.
> That number is so big it basically means nothing.

So I turned it into something you can actually *understand*.

---

## 🤖 What this is

A Custom GPT that lets you **chat with the U.S. government’s financial statements**.

Not summaries. Not opinions.
The actual report.

---

## 🧠 What it does

Instead of “$34 trillion”, you get:

* 💰 Your estimated share of the debt
* 🏠 The cost per household
* 📊 Real-world equivalents that actually make sense

You can ask things like:

* “How fast is the debt growing?”
* “What does this mean for inflation?”
* “Is this sustainable?”

## 📊 Where the data comes from

This is based on the official **U.S. Government Accountability Office (GAO)** report:

📄 [https://www.gao.gov/assets/gao-26-108073.pdf](https://www.gao.gov/assets/gao-26-108073.pdf)

It’s ~259 pages long.

Most people will never read it.

## 💡 Why this exists

Governments publish massive reports.

They’re technically public.
But practically unreadable.

So the question was:

> What if you could just *talk* to it instead?

---

## 👀 What makes this interesting

“Trillions” doesn’t register.

But:

* ~$100K per person
* ~$250K+ per household

That *does*.
